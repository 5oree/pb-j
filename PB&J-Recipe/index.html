PB&J Recipe!

Learn how to make a simple Peanut Buttter and Jelly Sandwich.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of bread
  • Peanut Butter
  • Jelly (Grape Jelly)

Instructions

  1. Lay out two slices of bread.
  2. Sperad Peanut butter on one slice.
  3. Spread jelly on the other slice.
  4. Put the two slices together.
